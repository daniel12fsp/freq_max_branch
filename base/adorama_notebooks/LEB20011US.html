About the Lenovo ThinkPad Edge E545

The Lenovo ThinkPad Edge E545 15.6" Notebook Computer will make a name for itself with the innovative 15.6" Screen Size.

Full-Sized Spill-Resistant Keyboard
The award-winning ThinkPad ergonomic keyboard is renowned for its full array of keys, great feel and TrackPoint pointing device. It also features a full-sized, spill-resistant design that protects the circuitry from liquids.

Enhanced Graphics
Enjoy beautiful, lifelike visuals from your E545 with AMD Radeon.

Fast Data Transfer
Move data quickly between the E545 and other devices with USB 3.0, which enables file transfer up to 10 times faster than previous USB technologies, allowing for lightning-fast copying of large media files, as well as seamless connections between audio- and video-related peripherals.

Drop-Down Hinges and Active Protection System
Enjoy a laptop that lasts, thanks to the durability of drop-down hinges and Active Protection System (APS), which detects when the system is dropped and reacts by stopping the hard drive and protecting your data.

Lightweight
Whether you are travelling around the world or commuting between offices, at just at 5.4 lbs, the E545 won't weigh you down.

Integrated 720p HD Webcam and Dual Noise-Cancelling Microphones
Never miss a thing in web conferences: low-light sensitive 720p HD webcam with wide-angle viewing and face-tracking, dual noise cancelling microphones with configurations for crystal-clear private and conference mode VOIP meetings, plus, convenient multimedia keys provide quick and easy access to microphone, speaker and camera controls.

15.6-Inch HD Display
The E545's HD display delivers bright, crisp visuals from any angle.

Dolby Advanced Audio v2
Enjoy an immersive surround sound experience whether listening to a presentation or kicking back with a movie. This technology increases audio clarity for VOIP applications, maximizes volume output without distortion and improves dialogue clarity when watching videos.
