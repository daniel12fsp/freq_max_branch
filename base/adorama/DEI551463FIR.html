About the Dell M531R

The Dell Inspiron M531R 15.6" HD Notebook Computer (Fire Red) features AMD Elite Accelerated Processor in a sleek design. So you get all the performance you need, with a little more room in your bag for the other things that matter.

Whether you're streaming blockbusters or setting a new high score, AMD Elite Accelerated Processor enhances your experience. Get the most from your favorite Windows 8.1 apps and games with its long battery life and stunning graphics. At about 1" thin, Inspiron M531R slips easily into a travel bag when you need to take your work with you.

The standard 65WHr 6-cell battery can help you stay powered up long enough to skip the in-flight movie, polish your presentation and drift off to your favorite music. Complete your home entertainment experience by connecting your Inspiron M531R to an external HDMI-capable TV or monitor via the HDMI 1.4 port.
