About the MSI GE70 Apache Pro-012

Blaze ahead with the sleek and slim MSI GE70 Apache Pro-012 17.3" Full HD Gaming Notebook Computer featuring the fast 4th generation Intel Core i7 processor and the powerful NVIDIA GeForce GTX 860M Series Kepler GPU. Boost your game performance with NVIDIA GPU Boost 2.0, extracting every inch of power for breathtaking visuals. Intelligently switch from the NVIDIA GPU to low-power integrated graphics with Optimus when you are not gaming. Combined, Optimus, GPU Boost and Kepler maximize system efficiency and gives the optimum level of performance at all times, automatically.

Gaming Keyboard By Steel series
There is no exception for error when it comes to serious gaming. Play flawlessly with extreme anti-ghosting allowing for simultaneous key presses and rapid response times. Game proficiently in darkness with the illuminated LED backlit keyboard.

Matrix Multi-Display
Transform your gaming space into the ultimate multimedia center with the Matrix Display. Multi-task like a pro with support of up to 3 independent displays, including the native notebook display, to work, watch and play all at the same time.

High-Performance Networking For Online Entertainment
Killer E2200 Intelligent Networking uses 'Qualcomm Atheros' exclusive Advanced Stream Detect, combining superior intelligence and control for PCs. Killer E2200 automatically classifies and prioritizes latency-sensitive network traffic for online games, HD video and high-quality audio for better control and fewer interruptions in your online experience.

Amp Up Your Audio
Gold plated audio jacks working in sync with an audio amplifier provides maximum sound fidelity for best and sonically accurate headset experience. Gain the advantage with Audio Boost.
