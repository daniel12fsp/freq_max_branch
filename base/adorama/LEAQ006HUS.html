About the Lenovo ThinkPad T440s

The Lenovo ThinkPad T440s 14" FHD 1080P Notebook Computer delivers both in terms of performance and mobility. The ThinkPad T440s combines a 3-cell internal battery with a 3-cell external battery for up to 8.4 hours per charge (battery life may vary). The ThinkPad T440s features a hybrid carbon-fiber reinforced plastic lid with a magnesium alloy chassis for a durable yet portable design.

The 14" widescreen display is backlit with LED lamps, which brighten faster, use less power and last longer than their CCFL counterparts. The screen has a 16:9 aspect ratio, which is also the standard for HDTVs. The Full HD 1920 x 1080 native resolution is supported by integrated Intel HD Graphics 4400. The native resolution of the ThinkPad T440s allows you to play back 1080p Full HD content without upscaling.

The system is powered by a 4th generation Haswell dual-core 2.1 GHz Intel Core i7-4600U processor. It also has 8GB of 1600 MHz DDR3L RAM, a 256GB solid state drive, a 4-in-1 media card reader, a fingerprint reader, 802.11a/b/g/n/ac Wi-Fi, Bluetooth 4.0, an integrated webcam and microphone. There are also built-in stereo speakers with Dolby Home Theater v4 technology.

Windows 7 Professional (64-bit) is the installed operating system. This version of Windows 7 Professional is preinstalled through the downgrade rights in Windows 8 Pro (64-bit).

Dual-Core 4th-Gen Haswell Intel Core i7 Performance
The Lenovo ThinkPad T440s houses a 4th generation Ha swell Intel Core i7-4600U processor. Its dual cores can reach a stock speed of 2.1 GHz. The processor can also be overclocked up to 3.3 GHz, thanks to Intel Turbo Boost 2.0 technology. The 4th generation Haswell processor uses low voltage to extend battery life without sacrificing performance. With a dedicated 4MB L3 cache, 8GB of DDR3L RAM and Intel Hyper-Threading technology, you'll be able to push the dual cores up to four virtual cores for quick and efficient multitasking.

14" Antiglare LED-Backlit IPS Display
The display also features a 16:9 aspect ratio and has a 1920 x 1080 native resolution, powered by integrated Intel HD Graphics 4400. The display also features in-plane switching (IPS) technology, which provides uniform color and wide viewing angles. The native resolution allows you to play back 1080p Full HD content natively without upscaling. The screen also has a matte, antiglare finish, which allows you to use the ThinkPad T440s in brightly-lit environments with ease. You can also output video to an external display via the Mini DisplayPort.

802.11ac Wi-Fi & Bluetooth 4.0
The Lenovo ThinkPad T440s supports 802.11ac Wi-Fi, which provides a high-speed connection to your home or office wireless network. The ThinkPad T440s is also backwards compatible with 802.11a/b/g/n Wi-Fi as well. There is also Bluetooth 4.0 technology for connecting to Bluetooth-compatible devices, such as a wireless mouse.

Solid State Drive Technology
The Lenovo ThinkPad T440s has a solid state drive, which provides faster read and write speeds than the traditional hard drive. It also does not have moving parts, which allows the solid state drive to be more durable and less prone to physical damage.

Dual Battery Setup
The Lenovo ThinkPad T440s uses a dual battery configuration. It has an internal 3-cell battery as well as an external 3-cell battery. The external battery can be swapped out for another when it is running low on power. With two 3-cell batteries, the Lenovo ThinkPad T440s can run for up 8.4 hours on a single charge. Please note that battery life may vary depending on usage.

USB 3.0 - 10x Faster than USB 2.0
The Lenovo ThinkPad T440s utilizes USB 3.0. USB 3.0 can reach data transfer speeds of up to 4.8 Gbps, which is up to ten times faster than USB 2.0. USB 3.0 is also backwards compatible with USB 2.0 devices so you don't have to worry about losing access to your older peripherals. Also, one of the USB 3.0 ports is always on for added convenience.

4-in-1 Media Card Reader
The Lenovo ThinkPad T440s has a 4-in-1 media card slot that can read Secure Digital (SD), Secure Digital High Capacity (SDHC), Secure Digital extended Capacity (SDXC) and MultiMediaCard (MMC).
