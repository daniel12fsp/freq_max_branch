About the Asus K550CA-EH51T



Asus K550CA-EH51T 15.6" HD Touchscreen Notebook Computer
The Asus K550CA-EH51T 15.6" HD Touchscreen Notebook Computer is built to meet your computing needs, and exceed your expectations. Featuring a slim, strong, lightweight chassis, at only 5.1 lbs. and powered by the Intel Core i5-3337U 1.8GHz processor it delivers fast performance. Multi-tasking capabilities are improved with 4GB RAM, making it ideal for your day-to-day tasks. It has a large touchpad equipped with Smart Gesture technology for a more intuitive experience. A 500GB HDD offers ample storage space for your important documents, and media files. Installed Windows 8 OS provides smooth, responsive performance for multitasking or multimedia consumption. Order the 15” LED HD ASUS Notebook now and enjoy working with interactive touch. Offered in new condition only, with free shipping!

From the Manufacturer

The Asus K550CA-EH51T 15.6" HD Touchscreen Notebook Computer redefines your expectations for an all-rounder notebook with its slim chassis coupled with an elegant concentric circle finish that keeps the notebook free from scratches and fingerprints. The K550 is also home to a powerful Intel Core processor as well as an optical drive that delivers smooth multitasking performance and the power to do more. Available with an Intel Core processor, the K550 delivers smooth, responsive performance for multitasking or multimedia consumption. Get up to speed faster with exclusive Super Hybrid Engine II featuring Instant On technology-allowing the notebook to resume from sleep mode in just two seconds. Data is also safe and secure with the K550 which automatically backs up your data when battery level drops below 5%.

A Classic, Timeless Design
The new K550 notebook features a concentric circle finish design which not only adds a classy and elegant touch to the notebook, but also helps to leave it free from fingerprints and scratches.

Video Calls in HD Clarity
Enjoy 16:9 ratio full screen video conferencing and improved low-light performance thanks to a highly sensitive sensor which automatically adjusts the brightness under low-light conditions.

Maximize your Windows 8 Experience
Touch screen display makes it easy to navigate using your fingers - tapping, swiping and double clicking like you would on a tablet.

Multi-Touch Made Easy
The K550 has a large touchpad equipped with Smart Gesture technology for a more intuitive computing experience. Smart Gesture technology sets a new standard for touchpad navigation by letting you pinch to zoom in and out of images, drag-and-drop items, scroll and swipe through webpages.

Speedy File Transfer
USB 3.0 has data transfer speeds up to 10 times that of USB 2.0, so all your files and content are copied and backed up more quickly.

Clearer Sound and Deeper Bass
Featuring exclusive Asus SonicMaster technology, the ASUS K550 delivers deeper and richer bass, a wider audio range and pristine clarity by employing larger speakers and specially designed chambers within the limited space of a notebook to allow sound waves to fully expand outwards. With proprietary software, ASUS AudioWizard, it also offers access to new dimensions of sound with selectable modes including music, movie and even game mode fitting audio needs based on your environment and preferences.

Access Your Data Anytime, Anywhere
With cloud-based Asus WebStorage, keep everything in sync and access all your documents, photos, music and videos from any internet-connected device you are on. K550 comes with free 32GB of WebStorage cloud space for three years.
