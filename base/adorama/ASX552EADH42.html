About the Asus X552EA-DH42

The Asus X552EA-DH42 15.6" HD Notebook Computer features a concentric circle finish design which not only adds a classy and elegant touch to the notebook, but also helps to leave it free from fingerprints and scratches. Enjoy 16:9 ratio full screen video conferencing and improved low-light performance thanks to a highly sensitive sensor which automatically adjusts the brightness under low-light conditions. The X552EA-DH42 has a large touchpad equipped with Smart Gesture technology for a more intuitive computing experience. Smart Gesture technology sets a new standard for touchpad navigation by letting you pinch to zoom in and out of images, drag-and-drop items, scroll and swipe through webpages.
