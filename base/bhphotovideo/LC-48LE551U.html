    More info from Sharp

    View full HD images with the black Sharp 48" Class LC-48LE551U AQUOS Full HD LED TV. This model features a native 1920 x 1080 resolution and has a 60 Hz refresh rate to help minimize distortion on moving content. The Sharp AQUOS LC-48LE551U has a 47.6" diagonal display and is equipped with a direct LED backlight for producing rich and vibrant images. This TV's 4,000,000:1 contrast ratio showcases its ability to handle bright and dark viewing material.

    Three HDMI and a USB port are on board for connecting high-resolution multimedia sources. There is also a component video interface that doubles as a composite video input for connecting lower resolution sources. An optical audio output is also built-in for outputting digital sound to an external audio system. As an ENERGY STAR qualified TV, this model was designed for reduced power consumption and energy efficiency.

    Consumer Electronics Control (CEC), Audio Return Channel (ARC), and Mobile High-definition Link (MHL) support over HDMI
    Down-firing 20W (10W + 10W) stereo speakers with virtual surround sound
    Removable stand and wall mountable
    Slim design at 2.6" deep without the stand
    Power Saving Mode and Auto Power
