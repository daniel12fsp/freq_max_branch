Great for space saving! The L5010W larder fridge comes in classic white and offers a generous 112 litres of capacity spread over three shelves, a full width salad drawer and door racks. You’ll have all the space you need to keep food stored effectively. It has auto-defrost which prevents the build up of ice in the compartment, and it comes with an A+ energy rating. Not only this, but it's endorsed by the Energy Saving Trust, so you know you're getting a really efficient appliance.

LEC L5010W

An example of a classic fridge, the Lec L5010W doesn’t come with complicated features and new fangled gadgets. Its 112 litre capacity speaks for itself. It has 3 glass safety shelves and a large salad bin. For a regular family, this is ample space for all your fresh food needs. The 3 door racks offer you a place to keep bottles of milk, yogurts and dairy product too.

There’s an interior light for easier navigation at night and don’t worry too much about cleaning because the auto-defrost will make sure there’s little ice build up.

It’s also convenient and efficient because the door is fully reversible to better aid placement and the A+ energy rating is one of the best on the market.

Finance is available on orders of £249 or more, placed on our website. This amount includes any additional services and delivery options selected, however does not take into account any promotions, such as cashback.

What do I need to apply?

All you need to do is fill out the online form at the payment stage and add an electronic signature. It’s important you use your full name and address details and make sure everything is spelt correctly. Please note, this is an online only finance option.

What happens if my application is turned down?

Applications may not be accepted for a number of reasons, including mis-typed information, or not meeting the specified requirements. Please note that other conditions may apply and we cannot guarantee that your application will be accepted.
