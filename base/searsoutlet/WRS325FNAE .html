Whirlpool 25.0 cu. ft. Side-by-Side Refrigerator w/ Accu-Chill™ Temperature Management - Energy-Saving and Adaptive Save energy and cool your food quickly with this Whirlpool side-by-side refrigerator WRS322FNA . The Accu-Chill Temperature Management System delivers efficient performance, using built-in sensors to measure the internal temperature and activate cooling processes only when necessary. Automatic adaptive defrost saves energy and gives you improved food preservation by reducing the formation of ice crystals on your frozen food. It features stainless steel handles and accents for all colors. Part of the white ice and black ice collections, this design will add a touch of strength and style to your kitchen. Available in stainless steel, white ice or black ice, the seamless exterior leaves the doors sleek and clean.

  • SpillGuard™ glass shelves contain leaks and spills for easier cleanup
  • Gallon door bins free up interior shelf space and allow you to store large containers in the door
  • Humidity-controlled crisper keeps produce fresh and organized
  • LED lighting in the refrigerator and freezer casts a natural light on your food and makes it easy to find what you’re looking for
