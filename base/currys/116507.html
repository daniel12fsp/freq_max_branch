Product details

The 16 GB Asus ZenFone 6 in black combines gorgeous design with a powerful camera, stunning quad HD screen resolution, multiple connectivity functions and much more.

Stylish and responsive

Defined by simple, clean lines and a light, elegant design, the Asus ZenFone 6 is a beautiful smartphone packed with the power to handle all your daily needs. It's driven by a 2.0 GHz Intel® Atom™ Multi-Core Z2580 Processor with Intel Hyper-Threading Technology, which allows you to multitask with ease.

This processor offers optimised performance and power efficiency, opening apps quickly, keeping games running smoothly and even letting you browse JavaScript-heavy web pages. Importantly, battery life isn't compromised, so you can stay mobile for longer.

Take amazing photos

Your ZenFone 6 includes a 13 MP rear camera powered by exclusive Asus PixelMaster technology that delivers brilliantly clear daytime shots, vibrant night-time photos and amazingly bright videos. PixelMaster combines software, hardware and optical designfor outstanding image quality for professional quality photos of every moment.

The camera is capable of a stunning 4128 x 3096 resolution, and image stabilisation has been built in as standard to make sure that all your photos look crisp and defined. The 4x zoom allows you to get closer to the action, and auto focus helps you to take more great-looking photos with less blurring.

A 2 MP front camera allows you to take easy selfies and makes sure you never miss a moment.

See everything on an HD display

Thanks to its high resolution 1280 x 800 6” HD IPS display, the ZenFone 6 is able to offer a maximum brightness of 400 nits that lets you see and read clearly, even when you're outdoors in bright sunlight.

Clarity, contrast and colour are all further enhanced by Asus TruVivid technology so that all your web pages, movies and photos look their best.

Tough touchscreen

An amazingly responsive touchscreen makes your Asus ZenFone 6 a joy to use with a ground-breaking response time of around 60 ms. It even offers accurate input and excellent general touch response when you're wearing gloves.

You'll experience virtually no lag while scrolling, and smudges are kept at bay by a durable anti-fingerprint coating so that your screen is always easy to see.

Corning Native Damage Resistance technology helps to improve surface density for a more durable screen with less danger of lateral cracking. The phone is also fitted with Corning Gorilla Glass 3 for scratch resistance bolstered by up to four times in comparison to standard Al-Si Glass and by ten times compared to Na-Si Glass.

Great signal and download speeds

An advanced antenna gives the Asus ZenFone 6 superior signal performance, even in areas with low network coverage. Precision aerial tuning reduces power consumption when the phone is searching for a signal, notably improving standby time.

HD video streaming, web browsing and using apps on the move is easy and seamless with support for network speeds up to 42 Mbps DC-HSPA+.

Multi-dimensional entertainment

The ZenFone 6 features a PowerVR SGX 544 MP2 GPU for serious performance, when you're gaming and for general use. With the all-new ZenUI, watching HD movies and playing 3D games is beautifully smooth and stutter free, making mobile entertainment truly immersive.

ZenUI is intuitively designed to make complex procedures simple and offers a genuinely personalised experience with its multi-coloured themes. Its intelligent technology allows you to use your phone the way you like, every day.

Asus SonicMaster technology delivers optimised audio courtesy of the Asus Golden Ear Team. It combines innovative hardware and software to bring you the ultimate sound experience.

With improved responsiveness and speed, an incredible camera and comprehensive entertainment features, the 16 GB Asus ZenFone 6 in black is the smartphone that does everything you need it to, when you need it done.
